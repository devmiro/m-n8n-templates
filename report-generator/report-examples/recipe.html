Mediterranean Herb-Crusted Salmon with Roasted Vegetables

Cuisine Type

Mediterranean

Difficulty Level

Intermediate

Prep Time

25 minutes

Cook Time

35 minutes

Serves

4

Ingredients

Main Protein

  • 4 salmon fillets (6 oz each)
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon sea salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper

Herb Crust

  • 1/2 cup fresh breadcrumbs
  • 2 tablespoons fresh parsley, chopped
  • 1 tablespoon fresh dill, chopped
  • 1 tablespoon fresh oregano, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons lemon zest

Roasted Vegetables

  • 2 medium zucchini, sliced
  • 1 red bell pepper, cut into strips
  • 1 yellow bell pepper, cut into strips
  • 1 red onion, sliced
  • 12 cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 3 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme

Cooking Instructions

Preparation

Preheat oven to 425°F (220°C)

Line two baking sheets with parchment paper

Pat salmon fillets dry and season with salt and pepper

Herb Crust Preparation

Combine breadcrumbs, herbs, garlic, and lemon zest in a bowl

Add 1 tablespoon olive oil to create a paste-like consistency

Press herb mixture firmly onto top of each salmon fillet

Vegetable Preparation

Cut all vegetables into uniform pieces for even cooking

Toss vegetables with olive oil, thyme, salt, and pepper

Spread vegetables in single layer on prepared baking sheet

Cooking Process

Roast vegetables for 15 minutes before adding salmon

Place herb-crusted salmon on second baking sheet

Cook both salmon and vegetables for additional 12-15 minutes

Salmon should flake easily and reach internal temperature of 145°F

Serving Suggestions

Accompaniments

  • Lemon wedges for extra citrus flavor
  • Tzatziki sauce or Greek yogurt
  • Warm pita bread or crusty sourdough
  • Mixed green salad with olive oil dressing

Wine Pairings

  • Sauvignon Blanc - crisp and herbaceous
  • Pinot Grigio - light and fresh
  • Rosé - complements the Mediterranean flavors

Storage and Leftovers

Refrigeration

Store leftovers for up to 3 days in refrigerator

Use airtight containers to maintain freshness

Reheat gently in 350°F oven for 8-10 minutes

Meal Prep Tips

  • Prepare herb crust mixture up to 2 days ahead
  • Cut vegetables the night before and store in refrigerator
  • Cook extra vegetables for easy weekday sides

Chef Notes

Substitutions

Fish Alternatives

  • Cod fillets
  • Halibut steaks
  • Sea bass

Herb Variations

  • Basil and thyme
  • Rosemary and sage
  • Cilantro and mint

Vegetable Swaps

  • Asparagus
  • Brussels sprouts
  • Eggplant

Pro Tips

  • Don't overcrowd vegetables on baking sheet for proper roasting
  • Let salmon rest at room temperature for 15 minutes before cooking
  • Use a meat thermometer for perfectly cooked salmon every time
